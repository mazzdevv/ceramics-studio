Who Are We?

Emma Thompson: The Visionary Dreamer

Emma Thompson, a visionary dreamer with a profound love for ceramics, is the heart and soul behind our community-based studio. With a background in fine arts and a deep appreciation for the beauty and versatility of clay, Emma set out to create a space where people from all walks of life could come together to explore their artistic potential.

Emma's vision for the studio was born out of a desire to foster a sense of belonging and creative expression. Her infectious enthusiasm and belief in the power of art as a means of self-discovery have been the driving force behind the studio's success. Through her tireless efforts, she has created an inclusive and nurturing environment where individuals can find solace, inspiration, and joy in working with clay.

Miguel Rodriguez: The Master Craftsman

Miguel Rodriguez, a master craftsman and ceramic artist extraordinaire, brings a decade of experience and expertise to our studio. His deep understanding of the technical aspects of ceramics, coupled with his passion for sharing his knowledge, has made him an invaluable asset to the community.

Miguel's journey with ceramics began at a young age, and over the years, he has honed his skills and mastered various techniques. His meticulous attention to detail and commitment to excellence are evident in every piece he creates. As a mentor and instructor at our studio, Miguel patiently guides aspiring artists, helping them unlock their creativity and develop their own unique style.

Shawn Johnson

Shawn Johnson, a natural-born community builder, is the glue that holds our ceramics studio together. With his warm and welcoming demeanor, Shawn has created a tight-knit community of artists who support and inspire one another. His belief in the power of collaboration and connection has turned the studio into a hub of creativity and friendship.

Shawn's background in community development has allowed him to create numerous opportunities for artists to come together and share their love for ceramics. From workshops and events to collaborative projects, Shawn ensures that the studio remains a vibrant and engaging space for everyone. He firmly believes that ceramics is not just an individual pursuit but a shared experience that is enriched through meaningful connections with others.

What do we do?

Our community ceramics studio is more than just a place to learn pottery—it's a space where artistic expression, personal growth, and connections flourish. Whether you're a seasoned ceramic artist looking to refine your skills, a beginner eager to embark on a creative journey, or someone seeking a welcoming community of like-minded individuals, our studio has something special to offer.

With a wide range of courses, classes, and social events, we provide an enriching and supportive environment for all levels of artists. Come join us as we explore the limitless possibilities of clay, nurture our creativity, and build lasting friendships. Together, we will shape beautiful works of art, forge memorable experiences, and make our mark in the world of ceramics.

Why do we do this?

In a world of digital connections and virtual experiences, the desire for tangible and hands-on activities has grown stronger than ever. Enter the world of ceramics—a mesmerizing art form that allows us to connect with the earth, express our creativity, and build meaningful connections with like-minded individuals. At our community-based ceramics studio, we have been fortunate to witness the transformative power of this ancient craft firsthand.

Through our shared vision and unwavering commitment, these founders have crafted a studio that is so much more than a place to create pottery—it is a place where dreams are realized, friendships are forged, and the transformative power of art is celebrated. Join us on this extraordinary journey as we shape clay, express ourselves, and embrace the joy of ceramics together.
